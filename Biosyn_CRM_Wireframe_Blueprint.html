Rules & behaviour

  • Session captures GPS at the first slide + server-authoritative start time; tracks every slide event, distinct slides seen, max slide.
  • RULE Valid visit = ≥5 slides + ≥30s + inside geofence + no spoofing. All four must pass or "End" is disabled.
  • RULE Geofence: AM 150 m / PM 100 m. Even 1 m outside = rejected.
  • Works fully offline; records queue with client UUID + client time + GPS and sync on reconnect.
  • RULE Cannot start a new session before ending the previous one and linking it to a doctor. Multiple products allowed in one visit.
